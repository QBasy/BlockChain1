About GovVoteChain

GovVoteChain is a decentralized application (dApp) designed to revolutionize government elections by ensuring enhanced security and transparency. The core idea is to leverage blockchain technology to create a tamper-resistant and auditable voting system.

Addressed Problem or Need:

Traditional voting systems face challenges such as fraud, manipulation, and lack of transparency. GovVoteChain addresses these issues by providing a secure, transparent, and decentralized platform for government elections.

Features

  • Blockchain Security
  • Transparent Ledger
  • Decentralized Identity Verification
  • Smart Contracts for Governance
  • Anonymity and Privacy
